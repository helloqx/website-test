Quick Help

The help button in the top right corner of each page generally provides useful tips on how to use that page.

Making Slides

You can make slides by going to 'Make Slides'. Find the song you need in the table and click on the '+' to add it to the list. After choosing your songs, click the 'Display' button to generate your slides.

Saving Slides

If you are logged in, your latest slides will automatically be saved upon displaying, and can be accessed from 'Your Slides'.

Adding Songs

If we do not have the song you want, you can easily add it by going to 'Add Song'. Please ensure that different slides are seperated with a blank line. Please do not add duplicate songs or spam the database :(.

Edit Songs

The Edit function is currently limited to admins only. Message me for the secret admin password, or simply add a corrected version again for now and tell me to delete the wrong song. Thanks.
